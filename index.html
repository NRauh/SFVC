    SFVC

    SFVC stands for simple, free video converter. It aims to be a simple and powerful tool to convert a wide array of video formats.

    Simple

    No need to worry about a load of settings, and things to configure; just select your file, and where you want the converted file to be saved to, and you're ready to go.

    Free

    Free as in free speech. Open source, and completely free to download, use, share, and modify.
