SFVC

Simple Free Video Converter

Simple

No need to configure a load of settings; just set your file, and select where you want to save the conversion to, then you're good to go.

Powerful

SFVC uses FFMpeg which allows it to convert to a wide array of formats with ease.

Free

Free as in speech. Open source, for anyone to download, use, share, modify, and contribute.
